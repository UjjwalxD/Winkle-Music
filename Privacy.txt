Winkle Music collects and stores the following information:

Server Information: We may collect information about the Discord servers where Winkle Music is used, such as server IDs, to improve our services and troubleshoot issues.

Usage Data: We may collect data on how you interact with the bot, such as the commands you use, songs you request, and playlists you create. This information helps us improve the bot's functionality and user experience.

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

Improving Our Services: We analyze usage data to identify trends, track bot performance, and enhance the overall user experience.

Customer Support: We may use your Discord user ID to provide support and assistance in resolving any issues or inquiries you may have.

Data Security

Winkle Music takes the security of your personal information seriously. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction of data.
